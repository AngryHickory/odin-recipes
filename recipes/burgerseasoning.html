Burger Seasoning

A Simple Seasoning for Burgers and other Meat

This is a simple seasoning made from common household spices for use on hamburgers and Other Meat.

How to Make Burger Seasoning

First we'll look at the ingredients, and then mix them together.

Burger Seasoning Ingredients

Youâ€™ll only need some basic spices for this recipe:

  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt

How to Make Burger Seasoning

There really isn't much to this recipe. Just add all the ingredients to a bowl and stir until mixed.

How Much Seasoning To Use

Season to taste. This recipe should make enough seasoning for about 1 lb of ground beef, or about three 1/3 lb patties.
